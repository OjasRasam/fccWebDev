Manchow Soup

I really like manchow soup, whether it is chicken or veg. And top it off with some fried noodles. That hits the spot!

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Ginger finely chopped
  • Garlic finely chopped
  • Capsicum finely chopped
  • Soy Sauce, Red and Green Chilli Sauce
  • Veg stock or chicken stalk
  • Corn Flower
  • Fried Noodles

Instructions

  1. Take a wok, put some oil and then saute the garlic and ginger quickly on high flame for smokey feel
  2. Add Capsicum
  3. Add Sauces
  4. Add Stalk
  5. Add water for consistency
  6. Add corn flour slurry
  7. While serving top it off with some nice crunchy fried noodles
